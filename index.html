Brief Bio:

I have joined the UBTech Robotics Research, as a senior engineer. I joined the southern university of science and technology (SUSTC) from 2019/07 to 2019/12, as a junior researcher. I received a Master's degree in Mechanical manufacturing and automation from Shanghai University, China, in 2019. My research interests include object detection and medical image analysis.
